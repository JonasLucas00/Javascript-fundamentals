BMI Interpretation — Adults (18+ years)

For adults, BMI classification is the same regardless of age or sex.

BMI Classification Obesity Grade
Below 18.5 Underweight 0
18.5 - 24.9 Normal weight 0
25.0 - 29.9 Overweight I
30.0 - 39.9 Obesity II
40.0 and above Severe Obesity III

BMI Interpretation — Children & Adolescents (2-19 years)

BMI interpretation for children depends on age and sex and requires growth charts.

BMI Percentile Classification
Below 5th percentile Underweight
5th - 84th percentile Normal weight
85th – 94th percentile Overweight
95th percentile or above Obesity
